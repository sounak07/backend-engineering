### **Day 0 – Arrival in Tokyo**

- **Arrival & Transport**: Land at Narita or Haneda, take train or airport limousine bus (~₹1,500–2,500).
    
- **Hotel Suggestion**:
    
    - Business hotels in Tokyo average **US$60 (~₹5,000)** per night[Time Travel Turtle](https://www.timetravelturtle.com/japan/is-japan-expensive/?utm_source=chatgpt.com).
        
    - For budget hostels: dorm beds ~~US$20 (~~₹1,600); private rooms ~~US$50 (~~₹4,200)[Never Ending Footsteps](https://www.neverendingfootsteps.com/cost-of-travel-japan-budget/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)[Time Travel Turtle](https://www.timetravelturtle.com/japan/is-japan-expensive/?utm_source=chatgpt.com).
        
- **Evening Activities**: Explore **Shibuya Crossing** and **Harajuku’s Takeshita Street** for street food.

### **Day 1 – Tokyo Highlights**

- **Morning**: Visit **Senso-ji Temple (Asakusa)** + eat **Nakamise Street snacks**.
    
- **Afternoon**: **Akihabara** (electronics & anime capital).
    
- **Evening**: Head to **Odaiba** — see the Statue of Liberty replica, visit **TeamLab Planets** (immersive digital art), plus shopping and night views.
    
- **Food**: Street food and casual meals cost ~₹500–1,500; mid-range restaurants ~₹1,500–3,000 per meal[www.bajajfinserv.in](https://www.bajajfinserv.in/japan-trip-cost-from-india?utm_source=chatgpt.com).
    

---

### **Day 2 – Tokyo’s Icons**

- **Morning**: **Tokyo Tower** (view the skyline).
    
- **Midday**: Stroll the **Imperial Palace Gardens**.
    
- **Afternoon**: Visit **Ueno Park & Zoo**, and shop at **Ameyoko Market**.
    
- **Optional**: Full-day at **Tokyo Disneyland or DisneySea**.
    
- **Meals**: Budget-friendly meals ~₹500–800; budget ₹1,000–2,000 per person per day.
    

---

---

### **Day 4 – Kawaguchiko → Matsumoto (via option to Kamikochi)**

- **Morning**: Enjoy Mt. Fuji views from Lake Kawaguchiko.
    
- **Travel**: Proceed to **Matsumoto**, with optional **Kamikochi** stop (mountain valley, hiking).
    
- **Stay in Matsumoto**: Accommodation typically ~~US$50 (~~₹4,200) per person; double occupancy ~~US$100 (~~₹8,400)[The Broke Backpacker](https://www.thebrokebackpacker.com/kyoto-vs-osaka/?utm_source=chatgpt.com).
    
- **Food**: Try local soba noodles or regional cuisine (~₹800–1,500).
    

---

### **Day 5 – Matsumoto → Takayama**

- **Morning**: Visit **Matsumoto Castle**.
    
- **Travel**: Head to **Takayama** (train or bus).
    
- **Explore**: Walk around **Takayama Old Town**, browse the **Miyagawa Morning Market**, and optionally visit **Shirakawa-go**.
    
- **Stay**: Guesthouses average **US$86 (~₹7,200)** per night[Never Ending Footsteps](https://www.neverendingfootsteps.com/cost-of-travel-japan-budget/?utm_source=chatgpt.com).
    
- **Food**: Try local Hida beef dishes or local vegetarian fare (~₹800–2,000).
    

---

### **Day 6 – Takayama → Kyoto**

- **Transport**: Take **Hida Wide View Express** → **Nagoya** → bullet train → **Kyoto**.
    
- **Evening in Kyoto**: Visit **Nishiki Market**, stroll through Gion district.
    
- **Hotel**: Hostels from US$50; double room in guesthouses around US$100 (~₹8,400)[The Broke Backpacker](https://www.thebrokebackpacker.com/kyoto-vs-osaka/?utm_source=chatgpt.com).
    
- **Food**: Street food at Nishiki Market (~~₹500–800) or vegetarian-friendly Japanese meals (~~₹1,000–1,500).
    

---

### **Day 7 – Kyoto Exploration**

- **Morning**: **Fushimi Inari Shrine** (early to beat crowds).
    
- **Midday**: **Kiyomizu-dera Temple**.
    
- **Afternoon**: **Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)**.
    
- **Evening**: **Arashiyama**—Bamboo Grove, Togetsukyo Bridge plus **Sagano Romantic Train** (scenic ride).
    
- **Food**: Sample tofu-based kaiseki, vegetarian ramen, or street snacks (~₹800–1,500).
    

---

### **Day 8 – Kyoto → Osaka**

- **Morning**: Optionally revisit attractions.
    
- **Travel**: Short train ride to **Osaka**.
    
- **Explore**: **Osaka Castle**, **Dotonbori & Namba**, **Tsutenkaku Tower**.
    
- **Stay**: In Osaka, double occupancy rooms ~~US$80 (~~₹6,700)[The Broke Backpacker](https://www.thebrokebackpacker.com/kyoto-vs-osaka/?utm_source=chatgpt.com).
    
- **Food**: Try **Okonomiyaki** or **Takoyaki**, non-veg and vegetarian options widely available (~₹500–1,500).
    

---

### **Day 9 – Osaka Day Trip/Amusements**

- **Option A**: Full day at **Universal Studios Japan**.
    
- **Option B**: Day trip to **Nara**: visit Todaiji Temple, Deer Park.
    
- **Food**: Osaka is street-food heaven; plan for ~₹1,000–2,000 for meals.
    

---

### **Day 10 – Final Day in Osaka**

- **Morning**: Shopping in **Shinsaibashi**, view from **Umeda Sky Building**.
    
- **Afternoon**: Depending on flight, either stay in the city or move near **Kansai Airport** (1 hour train).
    
- **Departure**: Fly home.
    
- **Food**: Last meals in Dotonbori—ramen, sushi, vegetarian-friendly options (~₹800–2,000).
